Verstein Andetier

Also known as the language of the angels, Verstein Andetier bares the closest resemblance to the language used up in Heaven by the angels and was brought down by the angels upon their descent down onto Earth. Although it is not as widespread or regularly used as its simplified version Alegvan Andetier, it is still commonly used in conversations within the High Houses in particular, however there are also a few in the Holy Houses who have learnt the language and have relative fluency in it.

It is said that the more 'pure' an angel's blood is, the easier it is for them to understand and speak Verstein Andetier, but this is not always the case and there are some exceptions. One such exception is Hoziah (), the apprentice of Moreh Japhael.

This is the language used by the Church for all ceremonial rituals and prayers.

Verstein Andetier is commonly referred to as simply "Verstein".

Alegvan Andetier

Alegan Andetier is the simplified version of Verstein Andetier. It was introduced as the official language of the Empire to not only unite the human population under one language, but also to provide a communcation bridge between the angels who descended and the humans on Earth.

The language is taught in educational institutions and it is the default primary language of use across the Empire, thus making most citizens fluent in the language. Citizens from more rural areas like the countryside may express better fuency in their Mother Tongue, which is the native language of their ancestors or the language previously used before the descent of the angels in the area they grew up in. However, it is more common for people to have Alegvan Andetier as their first language, while their Mother Tongue is considered their second language, though not all people take up their Mother Tongue at all. It is recommended by the government to retain at least a basic understanding of ones Mother Tongue as a means of honouring one's roots.

Alegvan Andetier is commonly referred to as simply "Alegvan".
