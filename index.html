synopsis

B efore their birth, Anis' purpose of existence had already been decided by the fates: to bring the people closer to the Great Almechting, to bring forth the Promised Revelation, to bring the awaited salvation to the lowly and the damned. Thus was their calling.

Or so they believed.

Upon the passing of their 25th birthday, things started to seem less clear, the doubts at the back of their head ring louder and the people around them seem darker than ever before. Perfection only exists for one supreme being and the rest are subjected to the befouled shades of black; nothing is ever truly just one way or the other, what is true good and what is true evil?

Is this really their reason for living? Are they really meant to be the bible for purity and righteousness? Is this all that they are supposed to be?

Because what if the holy...

aren't so holy after all?
